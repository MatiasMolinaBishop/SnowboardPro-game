RULES OF THE GAME:

Simples, use your powers to distroy energy balls & kill your enemies.
If you collect all 7 dragon balls you will win & a wish will be granted.
Don't let energy balls nor enemies touch you. You will die.

CONTROL CHARACTER WITH KEYBOARD:

Use the keyboard arrows to move your character.
Use the Z and X keys to use your long range powers
Hover over dragon balls with your character to colletc them
